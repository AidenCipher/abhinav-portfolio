# Resume Data Template

To personalize your website, please provide the following information from your resume:

## Contact Information
- **Email**: (e.g., your.name@email.com)
- **LinkedIn URL**: (e.g., https://linkedin.com/in/yourname)
- **Name**: (For site title/branding)

## Professional Summary / About
Please provide 3-5 paragraphs describing:
- Your professional focus and approach
- Key principles or philosophy that guides your work
- Your background and what drives you
- (Keep it essay-style, not resume format)

## Work Experience / Case Studies (2-3 projects)
For each case study, provide:

**Title**: Project/role name
**Context**: The situation/company/role
**Problem**: What challenge you were addressing
**Approach**: Your strategic approach to solving it
**Execution**: How you actually implemented the solution
**Outcome**: Measurable results and impact
**Reflection**: What you learned or key insights

## Writing / Publications (if any)
For each piece of writing:
- **Title**: Article/publication title
- **Description**: Brief description
- **Theme**: Category (e.g., Strategy, Technology, etc.)
- **Date**: Publication date (YYYY-MM-DD format)
- **URL**: Link to the writing (Medium, blog, etc.)

## Current Focus (Now Page)
- **Focus**: What you're currently focused on (2-3 items)
- **Learning**: What you're learning (2-3 items)
- **Exploring**: What you're exploring (2-3 items)
